About Sintok's Echoes

A safe, anonymous space for UUM students to share their stories, feelings, and experiences. We respect privacy and aim to provide useful analytics to help student services better understand campus sentiment while keeping individuals anonymous.

Our Mission

To provide an anonymous outlet for students to express concerns and for administrators to spot trends and take action without violating user privacy.
